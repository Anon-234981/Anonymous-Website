Past Operations of Anonymous

🇳🇬 Operation Nigeria (2013)

Defaced Nigerian government websites protesting harsh anti-LGBTQ laws.

🇳🇿 Operation GCSB (New Zealand)

DDoS attacks on GCSB and political websites after laws enabling citizen surveillance.

🇸🇬 Operation Singapore (2013)

Leaked over 3,600 emails, attacked multiple national websites in protest of censorship and government policies.

🇵🇭 Operation Philippines & Infosurge

115 websites defaced, others disabled after Typhoon Haiyan and PDAF scandal.

🇭🇰 Operation Hong Kong (2014)

Supported pro-democracy protestors; threatened cyber-attacks against the Hong Kong government.

🇷🇺 Operation Russia (2022–)

Major hacks targeting Russian government, banks, and state TV after Ukraine invasion.

🇺🇸 Operation Ferguson

Released personal data of police and government officials after the killing of Mike Brown.

🇺🇸 Operation KKK

Exposed identities of alleged Ku Klux Klan members and supporters.

🌐 Operation Death Eaters

Effort to expose international child exploitation and elite abuse networks.

🇧🇷 Operation Bolsonaro (2020)

Leaked personal info of Bolsonaro and allies in protest of authoritarianism.

📦 BlueLeaks (2020)

269GB of law enforcement data leaked, including police emails and sensitive documents.

🇺🇬 Operation Uganda (2020)

Took down police websites after crackdown on opposition candidate Bobi Wine.

🇮🇩 Operation Indonesia (2024)

Leaked personal info allegedly linking VP-elect Gibran Raka to a controversial online persona.
